  1. Dock: Your repository ties/clone at BSNP server. You can read/write.
  2. Undock: Your repository leaves BSNP server. Your data at BSNP server will be erased. No one can view your data anymore.
  3. Push: to push data to your repository during docked.
  4. Pull: to pull data from your repository during docked.
